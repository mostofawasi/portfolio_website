Polynomial Regression & Regularization Methods

"Polynomial Regression & Regularization Methods" refer to statistical techniques used to model non-linear relationships and prevent overfitting in regression analysis. Polynomial regression extends linear regression by adding polynomial terms of the predictors. Regularization methods, like Ridge and Lasso, add penalties to the model's coefficients to shrink them and improve generalization, especially when dealing with high-dimensional data or multicollinearity.
