🛒 Ecommerce Sales Dashboard

📌 Overview

The Ecommerce Sales Dashboard is a comprehensive tool designed to track **sales performance, revenue trends, product profitability, and customer behavior**. It helps businesses optimize their strategies by analyzing key sales data.

✨ Features

  • 📊 Total Revenue & Sales Analysis – Provides insights into sales performance.
  • 📦 Top-Selling Products – Lists the best-performing products based on revenue and sales.
  • ⏳ Units Sold by Hour – Shows sales distribution across different hours.
  • 📅 Date-Based Filters – Allows filtering sales data over custom time periods.
  • 📈 Refund & Sponsor Cost Monitoring – Tracks refunds and sponsorship spending.
